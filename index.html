Astra Malorum

I. INITIAL SETUP
Turn On Power
Follow cables from spawn to generator rooms. Activate all switches.
Unlock Pack-a-Punch
Requires activating all power nodes across the map.
Locate Star Fragments
Glowing purple shards found around the map (rooftops, ritual rooms, corridors).
II. BUILD ASTRA DEVICE
Collect 3 Astra Components
Found in hidden rooms and dropped by special enemies.
Assemble Device at Ritual Table
Central altar location. Interact to build.
Charge Device with Souls
Kill zombies near it until fully glowing purple.
III. SYMBOL PUZZLE
Find Wall Symbols
Look for glowing glyphs around the map.
Match Symbols to Device
Input correct order based on symbols found.
Activate Sequence
Wrong order resets the puzzle.
IV. TRIALS
Enter Trial Portal
Appears after puzzle completion.
Complete Combat Challenges
Examples: survive waves, melee kills, defend zones.
Repeat for All Trials
Usually 3 total.
V. ORB CHARGING
Locate Floating Orbs
Purple energy spheres around map.
Shoot with Wonder Weapon
Regular guns won’t work.
Charge Each Orb
Kill zombies near orb until it explodes.
VI. FINAL RITUAL
Return to Ritual Table
Device should now be fully powered.
Start Final Sequence
Triggers boss fight immediately.
Boss Fight
Recommended:
- Pack-a-Punch Tier 3
- Wonder Weapon upgraded
- Full armor
Defeat Boss → Complete EE
Avoid standing still — constant movement required.
