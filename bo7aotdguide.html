Ashes of the Damned EE Guide

1. Setup + Power
Get Tedd’s Head from spawn crate
Repair Ol' Tessie (truck)
Go to Vandorn Farm OR Blackwater Lake
Clear Power Pump (destroy orange blobs fast)
Activate Power Switch
Go to Ashwood and repeat power process
2. Pack-a-Punch + Early Items
Drive Tessie to garage
Install Pack-a-Punch on vehicle
Grab Jar of Spores (Lake cabin)
Interact with dead horse (Farm)
Return after 3 rounds → get Widow’s Lantern
3. Vehicle Upgrade + Parts
Kill Cook in diner → get Freezer Key
Open freezer → get Abomination Carcass
Upgrade Tessie in garage
Destroy purple plants → collect Hoard Husk
Use Saw Trap during Ravager round → get Eyes
4. Mid Game Progression
Collect Human Bones
Defeat Uber Klaus (Round ~8)
Revive Klaus at Sheriff Office
Use Klaus to get Aether Barrel
Charge Aether Barrel
Unlock Wonder Weapon (Necrofluid Gauntlet)
5. Ritual + Keys
Collect Powder of the Forgotten
Get Mysterious Limbs
Solve Yuri’s Lab puzzle
Perform Blood Sacrifice
Collect 3 Keys in fog
6. Rocket + Towers
Install DG-2 turret barrel on car
Charge Gauntlet using clock tower
Shoot both spinning radio towers
Go to Cosmodrome control room
Input correct launch code
Launch rocket
7. Lightning Step
Charge Gauntlet (clock tower)
Shoot rooftop clock → pause lightning
Spawn 3 special zombies:
• Waitress (cash register)
• Engineer (Klaus stomp)
• Trucker (ram red truck)
Lead each zombie to matching lightning
8. Final Steps
Collect artifacts at farm
Shoot film reels at lake
Collect souls in fog (POINT OF NO RETURN)
Prepare for boss fight
